Surfing Vacation Itinerary



Week 1
June 16 - 20
 Surfers will learn about Internet Safety while developing their searching skills. They will begin learning how to use Microsoft Word & PowerPoint. Plus, they will be introduced to and begin reading the E-Wally and the Quest book.
Week 2
June 23 - 27
 Surfers will finish learning how to use Microsoft PowerPoint. They will be introduced to scanning and using digital cameras. We will finish reading E-Wally and the Quest. Plus, they will plan and create a storyboard for their group PowerPoint project.
Week 3
June 30 - July 3
 Surfers will take their storyboards, which they created in groups last week, and make a group PowerPoint to present to the class on Thursday or Monday. They will also plan and begin a storyboard for their individual project.
Week 4
July 7 - 11
 Surfers will complete their storyboard for their individual projects. Then they will create and present an individual PowerPoint presentation.
